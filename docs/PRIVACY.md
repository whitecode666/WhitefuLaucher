# FufuLauncher 隐私政策 (Privacy Policy)

**生效日期：** 2026年6月3日

欢迎您使用 FufuLauncher（以下简称“本软件”）。我们深知个人隐私对您的重要性，并致力于保护您的个人信息安全。本《隐私政策》旨在以专业、透明的方式向您详细说明我们在您使用本软件期间的数据处理规则，包括我们如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的数据。

在使用本软件前，请您务必仔细阅读并理解本政策。

***

## 1. 信息的收集与使用

本软件的核心设计理念是“本地优先”。我们最大程度地限制了对用户数据的收集，并确保您的核心业务数据完全由您自行掌控。

### 1.1 本地处理与存储的数据

您在使用本软件过程中产生的以下数据，**均仅在您的设备本地进行处理和加密存储**，我们不会将其上传至任何外部服务器：

- **账号与凭证信息：** 包括但不限于您的游戏账号标识、登录 Token、Cookie 及相关身份验证凭证。
- **游戏与使用数据：** 包括您的游戏本地设置、抽卡记录导出数据、游戏时长统计、应用内个性化配置（如主题、快捷键等）。
- **本地文件与路径：** 游戏安装目录、截图文件夹及其他相关的本地系统路径信息。

### 1.2 自动收集的错误日志 (Sentry)

为了持续改善软件的稳定性、快速定位并修复程序缺陷（Bug），本软件集成了 **Sentry** 作为崩溃和错误收集服务。

- **收集触发条件：** 仅当本软件发生未处理的异常、崩溃或严重错误时触发。
- **收集的数据范围：**
  - 设备的非个人身份信息（如操作系统版本、设备型号、系统架构等）。
  - 软件运行状态（如软件版本号、运行时环境）。
  - 崩溃发生时的堆栈跟踪（Stack Trace）及上下文代码信息。
- **使用目的：** 收集的日志信息仅用于技术团队进行故障排查和代码优化，**绝对不包含**您的账号凭证、游戏内敏感数据或其他可识别您个人身份的信息。

***

## 2. 数据上传的特别声明（核心承诺）

我们在此向所有用户做出庄重且明确的承诺：
**除上述通过 Sentry 收集必要的、匿名的崩溃诊断日志外，FufuLauncher 没有任何其他主动将用户数据上传、发送或同步至我们的服务器或任何第三方服务器的行为。**
您的隐私数据、游戏进度和账号安全完全由您的本地设备环境保障。

***

## 3. 信息的共享与披露

由于我们并未在云端收集和存储您的任何个人业务数据，因此我们**不存在**向任何第三方出售、交易或共享您个人信息的情况。

- **第三方服务商：** 仅有提供错误追踪服务的 Sentry 会接收到匿名的崩溃日志数据，且其数据处理严格遵循 Sentry 的隐私合规标准。
- **法律要求：** 即使在面临法定机构的数据调取要求时，由于本软件的本地化特性，我们也无法提供您的任何本地存储数据。

***

## 4. 数据的存储与安全

- **数据控制权：** 您的所有核心数据均保存在您安装本软件的本地目录或系统指定的应用数据目录中。

## 5. 您的权利

依据相关隐私保护法律法规，您对自己的数据拥有绝对的控制权：

- **访问与修改：** 您可以通过软件界面直接访问、修改您的各项配置和保存的数据。
- **删除与撤回：** 如前所述，您可以随时通过删除本地文件来行使您的数据删除权。如您不希望产生任何错误日志收集，您可以选择停止使用本软件。

# FufuLauncher Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 3, 2026

Welcome to FufuLauncher (hereinafter referred to as "the Software"). We deeply understand the importance of personal privacy to you and are committed to protecting the security of your personal information. This "Privacy Policy" aims to explain our data processing rules in a professional and transparent manner during your use of the Software, including how we collect, use, store, and protect your data.

Before using the Software, please be sure to carefully read and understand this policy.

***

## 1. Information Collection and Use

The core design philosophy of the Software is **"Local First"**. We limit the collection of user data to the greatest extent and ensure that your core business data is entirely under your control.

### 1.1 Data Processed and Stored Locally

The following data generated during your use of the Software is **only processed and encrypted locally on your device**, and we will not upload it to any external servers:

- **Account and Credential Information:** Including but not limited to your game account identifiers, login Tokens, Cookies, and related authentication credentials.
- **Game and Usage Data:** Including your local game settings, gacha record export data, playtime statistics, and in-app personalized configurations (such as themes, shortcuts, etc.).
- **Local Files and Paths:** Game installation directories, screenshot folders, and other related local system path information.

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Error Logs (Sentry)

To continuously improve the stability of the Software and quickly locate and fix program defects (Bugs), the Software integrates **Sentry** as a crash and error collection service.

- **Collection Triggers:** Triggered only when the Software encounters unhandled exceptions, crashes, or critical errors.
- **Scope of Collected Data:**
  - Non-personally identifiable device information (e.g., operating system version, device model, system architecture, etc.).
  - Software runtime status (e.g., software version number, runtime environment).
  - Stack traces and contextual code information when a crash occurs.
- **Purpose of Use:** The collected log information is solely used by the technical team for troubleshooting and code optimization, and **absolutely does not contain** your account credentials, sensitive in-game data, or other information that could personally identify you.

***

## 2. Special Declaration on Data Upload (Core Commitment)

We make a solemn and clear commitment to all users here:
**Except for the necessary, anonymous crash diagnostic logs collected through Sentry as mentioned above, FufuLauncher does not engage in any other active uploading, sending, or syncing of user data to our servers or any third-party servers.**
Your privacy data, game progress, and account security are fully guaranteed by your local device environment.

***

## 3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

Since we do not collect or store any of your personal business data in the cloud, we **do not** sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties.

- **Third-Party Service Providers:** Only Sentry, which provides error tracking services, will receive anonymous crash log data, and its data processing strictly complies with Sentry's privacy compliance standards.
- **Legal Requirements:** Even when facing data retrieval requests from legal authorities, due to the localized nature of the Software, we are unable to provide any of your locally stored data.

***

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- **Data Control:** All your core data is saved in the local directory where you installed the Software or the application data directory specified by the system.
- **Security Advice:** Please take good care of your personal computer devices and prevent the intrusion of malware or viruses to ensure the safety of your local data.
- **Data Cleanup:** You can thoroughly and permanently erase all data left by the Software on your device by uninstalling the Software and deleting the relevant local configuration file directories.

***

## 5. Your Rights

According to relevant privacy protection laws and regulations, you have absolute control over your data:

- **Access and Modification:** You can directly access and modify your various configurations and saved data through the software interface.
- **Deletion and Withdrawal:** As mentioned above, you can exercise your right to data deletion at any time by deleting local files. If you do not wish to generate any error log collection, you can choose to stop using the Software.

***

## 6. Protection of Minors

The Software is primarily intended for adult gamers and users with full capacity for civil conduct. If you are a minor, please use the Software under the guidance and with the consent of your legal guardian. Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, we do not actively obtain or verify users' age information.

***

## 7. Policy Updates

With the iteration of project features, updates to laws and regulations, or improvements in security standards, we may revise this "Privacy Policy" in due course.

- For any material changes, we will make a prominent announcement in the project's GitHub code repository (e.g., Releases page or README file).
- Continuing to use FufuLauncher after this policy is updated constitutes your acknowledgment that you have fully read, understood, and accepted the updated policy terms.

***

## 8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, comments, or suggestions regarding this privacy policy or our data processing practices, or if you need to report a security vulnerability, please contact us through the following channels:

- **Project Open Source Homepage:** Welcome to submit your feedback and questions via the GitHub Issues of this project, and the open-source community maintainers will answer them.
